Got questions?

  1. How do I create a playlist?

    2. Playlists are a great way to save collections of music, either for your own listening or to share.

    To create one:

    1. 1. Tap Your Library.
    2. 2. Tap

      CREATE

      .
    3. 3. Give your playlist a name.
    4. 4. Start adding songs (and we'll help you along).

  2. How do I activate Data Saver mode?

    1. 1. Tap

      Home

      .
    2. 2. Tap

      Settings

      .
    3. 3. Tap

      Data Saver

      .
    4. 4. Switch on Data Saver.

  3. Is it only possible to shuffle play music?

    4. Any playlist with the shuffle icon will play on shuffle.

    Some playlists won't have the shuffle icon, so you can tap any song to play it.

  4. Where can I find Podcasts?

    5. Tap

    Search

    . Under

    Browse All

    , tap Podcasts.
